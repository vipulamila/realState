Our Mission

EstateX was founded with a single goal: to make the property search and listing process as seamless, transparent, and premium as possible. We believe that finding a home is one of the most important journeys in life, and it should be treated with the elegance and efficiency it deserves.

Why EstateX?

Unlike traditional listings sites, we focus on quality over quantity. Every broker on our platform is verified, and every listing is curated to ensure high standards. Our "Gold Standard" verification process means you can trust the information you see.

Our Journey

Starting with just a few premium listings in Mumbai, EstateX has grown to cover 15+ major cities across India. We continue to innovate with features like our smart search, integrated broker communication, and seamless listing tools.
